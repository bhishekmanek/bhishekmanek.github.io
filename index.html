About Research

About

I am a Research Scientist at the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics (LASP), University of Colorado Boulder. My research combines solar physics, magnetoconvection, and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on video super-resolution and scientific computing. I am passionate about open science, AI-accelerated workflows, and bridging physics-based models with modern machine learning.

Research

OpenDopp

An open-source pipeline for super-resolution and cross-calibration of solar Dopplergrams from instruments like HMI, MDI, and GONG. Integrates vision transformers and patch-based data handling.

Magnetoconvection Modeling

Simulating and analyzing convective transport in the presence of magnetic fields using high-resolution MHD solvers.

ML for Helioseismology

Applying data-driven and deep learning methods to interpret helioseismic signatures and understand sub-surface solar dynamics.
